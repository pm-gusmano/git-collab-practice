Prerequisites:

- gainful employment
- access to a grocery store
- access to kitchen/kitchen utensils, espescially knives


Instructions

1. acquire bread loaf, jelly, and nut spread of choice at grocery store

2. set down bread upon counter

3. taking utensil of choice, spread nut spread and jelly across the long face of each slice of bread.

4. put faces of bread slices together so that nut spread and jelly are in contact.

5. admire and enjoy by eating!
